## Shooting / outside scoring
- **Deadeye** — loses less accuracy when a defender contests his jumper.
- **Limitless Range** — no penalty for shooting from way behind the arc.
- **Catch & Shoot** — bonus accuracy on a shot taken right off a pass.
- **Corner Specialist** — shoots better from the corners specifically.
- **Heat Check** — after a make, his next shot this game gets a one-time boost.
- **Movement Shooter** — better at shooting after running off a screen.
- **Free Throw Merchant** — drawn fouls convert to points more reliably.
- **Quick Release** — harder to block; help defense arrives too late.

## Finishing / inside scoring
- **Dream Shake** — can score a rim-quality shot from midrange (post footwork).
- **Eurostep** — finishes through contact at the rim, beating help defenders.
- **Lob Threat** — a pass to him at the rim is a near-automatic finish.
- **Putback** — extra chance to score his own team's missed shot.
- **Posterizer** — dunks that intimidate: a made dunk lowers the defense next possession.
- **Soft Touch** — better at floaters/runners in the in-between zone.
- **And-One** — small chance a made layup adds a bonus point.

## Playmaking / passing
- **Dimer** — teammates shoot better on shots created by his pass.
- **Floor General** — slightly raises every teammate's offense while he's on the floor.
- **Lob Passer** — his passes to the rim are safer and set up easy finishes.
- **Pace Setter** — his team gets a small chance at an extra fast-break possession.
- **Tempo Control** — lowers his team's turnover risk across the board.
- **Outlet Pass** — a defensive rebound by his team can launch a quick score.

## Ball-handling / creation
- **Killer Crossover** — breaks down the first defender, removing their help.
- **Handles for Days** — very low chance to turn it over when he initiates.
- **Iso Threat** — better shot quality when he creates his own look.
- **Draw Two** — forces a double-team, freeing up a teammate (chemistry hook).

## Defense — on ball
- **On-Ball Menace** — his man shoots worse even from a distance.
- **Lockdown** — heavily reduces his matchup's scoring.
- **Point of Attack** — shuts down the opposing ball-handler's drive.
- **Shot Blocker** — chance to swat a rim attempt by his man.
- **Rim Protector** — suppresses ALL shots near the basket, not just his man's.
- **Verticality** — contests without fouling; no free points given up.

## Defense — off ball / steals
- **Pickpocket** — high chance to steal in his man's passing lane.
- **Interceptor** — jumps passing lanes anywhere near him, not just his man.
- **Help Defender** — adds extra help pressure when he sags off his man.
- **Switch Everything** — no penalty when guarding a different position (mismatch-proof).
- **Charge Taker** — chance to turn an opponent's rim drive into a turnover.

## Rebounding
- **Glass Cleaner** — boosts his team's chance to grab missed shots.
- **Box Out** — denies the opponent second-chance points.
- **Tip-Out** — keeps offensive rebounds alive for a reset possession.

## Hustle / intangibles
- **Motor** — small all-around boost that grows late in a game.
- **Energy Guy** — gives a brief team boost when he checks in (off the bench, later).
- **Glue Guy** — boosts the WORST stat on the team by a little.
- **Vocal Leader** — reduces the whole team's turnover risk.

## Clutch / situational
- **Closer** — better in the final possessions of a close game.
- **Cold Blooded** — immune to pressure on game-deciding shots.
- **Slow Starter** — weaker early, ramps up as the game goes (could be a drawback).
- **Giant Slayer** — bonus when his team is the underdog (lower total rating).
- **Rivalry** — bonus vs a specific archetype or opponent style.

## Meta / economy / team-building
- **Star Power** — grants extra cap space while he's on your roster (your example).
- **Fan Favorite** — extra reward/cap on a win when he's in the lineup.
- **Mentor** — an adjacent low-level teammate levels up faster.
- **Recruiter** — improves the quality of players that appear in your market.
- **Loyalty** — gets cheaper to keep / cheaper to level the longer you hold him.
- **Throwback** — costs less but can't be leveled past L2.
- **Two-Way Contract** — very cheap, but no ability until you level him up.
- **Workhorse** — never tires; ignores any late-game fatigue penalties.

## Negative / drawback abilities (risk-reward players)
- **Turnover Prone** — great scorer, but noticeably higher turnover risk (your example).
- **Ball Stopper** — scores well but lowers teammates' offense (no ball movement).
- **Streaky** — bigger swings: hotter makes, colder misses than his rating suggests.
- **Sieve** — strong on offense, but his man scores more easily.
- **Locker Room** — strong stats, but slightly drags team chemistry/duos.
- **Injury Prone** — high ceiling, small chance to "sit" a game and miss possessions.
- **Cap Hog** — elite, but eats extra cap space (opposite of Star Power).
- **Heat Seeker** — only effective when fed; does nothing if not the first option.

## Duos & Big-3 chemistry (grounded in real combos)

When the named pair/trio share a lineup, apply the effect. Default per your spec: **+1 to all
four stats**. Some are flavored mechanics instead — mark which style you want per combo.

### Duos
- **Inside-Outside** (rim Big + scoring guard) — *Shaq & Kobe.* Both +1 all stats; the guard's
  drive-and-kick to the big is boosted.
- **Pick-and-Roll** (pass-first PG + rim/screen Big) — *Stockton & Malone.* The lob/roll finish
  off this PG's pass is near-automatic.
- **Splash Brothers** (two shooters) — *Curry & Klay.* Each ignores the other defender's help —
  the floor is too spread to double.
- **Lockdown Wings** (two elite defenders) — *defensive backcourt.* Opponent turnover risk up
  team-wide.
- **Twin Towers** (two bigs) — *Robinson & Duncan.* Big rim-protection + rebounding boost.
- **Veteran Backcourt** (PG + SG) — steady chemistry: lowers team turnover risk.

### Big 3s
- **Three-Level Trio** (slasher + shooter + rim-runner) — covers every scoring zone; whole team
  +1 OFF.
- **Position-less Big 3** (three versatile wings) — *LeBron/Wade/Bosh, Warriors core.* All three
  +1 all stats; switch-everything on defense.
- **Spurs Model** (PG + wing + anchor Big) — balanced +1 all, plus a small clutch bonus.
